I am a passionate educator, working with children, young adults, and fellow educators for the last 11 years.

I create transformative learning journeys using experiential methodologies, including theater, nature-based explorations, and interdisciplinary projects.

My work is deeply inspired by the philosophies of J. Krishnamurti, Martin Buber , and the science of brain, learning, and child development.

I focus on building genuine relationships with children, fostering life skills, ecological consciousness, and exploring the Panchkosha model of human development.

I am part of the core team for the ‘Child Development, Learning & Special Needs’ course at Eklavya Foundation and a proud recipient of the Earth Educator Fellowship (2022-23), supported by the Youth Conservation Action Network.
